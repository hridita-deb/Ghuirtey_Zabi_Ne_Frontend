About Us

Your Travel Companion for Exploring Chattogram

“Ghuirte Jabi Ne?” is a student-created travel guide designed specially for people who are visiting Chattogram from outside the city.

Our goal is simple — to bring together all the beautiful places of Chattogram in one website so that travelers don’t have to search everywhere. From beaches, hills, waterfalls, historical spots to hidden gems — everything is organized with descriptions, photos, and maps to make trip planning easier.

This project is developed by four undergraduate students of East Delta University: Halima, Sigma, Hridita, and Mimfa. Our mission is to help visitors explore the true beauty of Chattogram comfortably and confidently.
