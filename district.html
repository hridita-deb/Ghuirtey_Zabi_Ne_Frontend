Chittagong Division Districts

Chattogram

Chattogram is the commercial capital of Bangladesh, known for its rich history, port, hills, and beaches. The city blends tradition with modern urban culture.

Cox’s Bazar

Home to the world’s longest natural sea beach, Cox’s Bazar is the most iconic tourist destination in Bangladesh.

Bandarban

Bandarban is famous for its mountains, tribal culture, waterfalls, and the highest peaks in Bangladesh.

Rangamati

Known as the ‘Lake City’, Rangamati is famous for Kaptai Lake, tribal heritage, and scenic hills.

Khagrachari

The greenest hill district, popular for Alutila cave, hills, and tribal lifestyle.

Feni

Feni is known for its historical significance, rivers, and agricultural culture.

Noakhali

Noakhali is famous for its coastal environment, food culture, and the island of Nijhum Dwip.

Lakshmipur

Lakshmipur is a peaceful district with beautiful rivers and rural landscapes.

Chandpur

Famously known as the ‘Hilsa Capital’, Chandpur is located along the Meghna river.

Brahmanbaria

This district is known for its heritage, music tradition, and natural beauty.

Cumilla

Cumilla is rich in history, especially for Shalban Bihar, Mainamati, and its vibrant culture.
